How It Works

Our tiffin service is designed to make your life easier. Here's how it works:

  1. Choose the plan that works best for you
  2. Select the dishes you want for each meal
  3. Receive your tiffin box at your doorstep at the designated time

FAQs

  • What is a tiffin service?

    A tiffin service is a meal delivery service that provides fresh and healthy meals in a tiffin box.

  • Do you offer different kind of vegetarian options?

    Yes, we offer both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options for all our meal plans.

  • Can I customize my meals?

    Yes, you can customize your meals by selecting the dishes you want for each meal.

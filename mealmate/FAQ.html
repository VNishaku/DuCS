Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a tiffin service?

    A tiffin service is a food delivery service that provides home-cooked meals to customers on a regular basis.

  • How do I order from your tiffin service?

    You can place an order on our website by selecting the meals you want and providing your delivery information.

  • What types of meals do you offer?

    We offer a only variety of vegetarian meals that change daily. Check our menu page for more information.

  • How much do your meals cost?

    Our meals range from Rs.40 to Rs.300 depending on the options you select.
